Time Format Help

Day codes: M(Mon), T(Tue), W(Wed), R(Thu), F(Fri), S(Sat), U(Sun)

Period codes: 1-9, n(noon), a-d(evening)

Example: T3T4R3R4 = Tuesday 3rd-4th period + Thursday 3rd-4th period
